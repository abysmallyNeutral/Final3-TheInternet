W3C and HTML and CSS

The W3C, or the World Wide Web Consortium, is the main international standards organization for the World Wide Web. Founded in 1994 and currently led by Tim Berners-Lee, the combination of companies is made up of member organizations that maintain full-time staff working together in the development of standards for the World Wide Web. W3C also engages in education and outreach, develops software and serves as an open forum for discussion about the Web.
